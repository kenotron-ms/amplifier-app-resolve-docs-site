## The platform in one paragraph

You submit a **task** as an [instance](/resolve/get-started/concepts/) targeting a registered [resolver](/resolve/build/resolver-protocol/). The platform spawns an isolated Incus container, runs your resolver's `run()` inside it, and streams a live [event feed](/resolve/build/events/) back to you. When the resolver finishes it delivers an artifact — typically a pull request. Run a [reality check](/resolve/use/reality-check/) to verify the result actually works before merging.
